About

Unleash Your Inner Hero with Dumbbells & Dragons!

Step into the ultimate gamified fitness experience! Dumbbells & Dragons transforms your workouts into epic quests, helping you build barbarian-level strength while having a blast. Whether you’re crushing it in the gym or leveling up your skills, this app makes fitness fun, engaging, and rewarding.

Key Features:

  • Track Your Workouts: Monitor progress and stay motivated on every adventure.
  • Class-Based Training: Choose your hero class and enjoy tailored workouts designed to boost your strength, agility, and endurance.
  • Custom Workouts: Build your own routines and create a personalized class to suit your goals.
  • Complete Quests: Conquer challenges and earn rewards for your real-life fitness achievements.
  • Level Up Your Character: Gain experience and grow stronger as you hit your workout milestones.
  • Compete: Challenge friends or other players and prove who’s the ultimate hero.

Lore - The Fitness Challenge

In a world where health and energy are precious, every step, rep, and workout generates energy that strengthens not just the body, but your personal progress. Skipping workouts slows your momentum, while consistent effort powers you forward and unlocks new challenges.


As a user of the app, you are a Champion of Momentum. Every workout fuels your progress and helps you achieve more—both in the app and in real life.
